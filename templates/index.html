    How to Play Minesweeper

    Objective

    The objective of Minesweeper is to clear the board containing hidden mines without detonating any of them.

    Game Rules

    • Left Click: Reveal a cell
    • Right Click: Place or remove a flag
    • Numbers indicate how many mines are adjacent to that cell
    • Use the numbers to deduce where mines are located
    • Flag all mines to win the game

    Difficulty Levels

    • Beginner: 9x9 grid with 10 mines
    • Intermediate: 16x16 grid with 40 mines
    • Expert: 16x30 grid with 99 mines

    Game Display

    • Mine Counter: Shows remaining mines (total mines - flags placed)
    • Smiley Face: Click to restart the game
    • Timer: Shows elapsed time in seconds

    Tips

    • Start by clicking in the center or corners
    • Look for patterns in the numbers
    • When in doubt, flag suspicious cells
    • Empty cells will automatically reveal adjacent cells
